Having trouble signing in? Get help

Can't sign in?

Your account is managed by your company administrator. Gambit TSL does not support self-registration.

  • Make sure you're using the username given to you by your admin — not your email address.
  • Passwords are case-sensitive. Check that Caps Lock is off.
  • If this is your first login, use the temporary password provided and you'll be asked to change it immediately.
  • If you've forgotten your password, ask your company admin to reset it for you.
  • On mobile, ensure you have a stable internet connection before logging in.
Need further assistance? Contact your company administrator or reach Basilmark Support at support@basilmark.co.zw
